Training:

  • 3 Pillars of Gains: Training + Diet + Recovery
  • Dumbbells for athleticism, Machines/Cables for hypertrophy and bodybuilding, Barbell is a good mix of everything and for power/olympic lifting
  • Control eccentric and isometric (concentric control doesn't matter as much), don't use momentum to compensate heavy weights
  • Understand the difference between types of sets (Regular, Pyramid, Superset, Dropset, etc.)
  • High reps for hypertrophy low reps for strength
  • Rest days are very important don't skip (unless you train weak then ig you can skip rest days)
  • Progressive overload and mechanical tension <- there may be new science that disproves this but I'm too lazy to check rn
  • Train for a purpose, don't just go til the pump (also training to failure is overrated and pretty negligible tbh)
  • Warm up to be literally warmer (temperature affects performance)
  • Make sure you have consistent or improving volume to get stronger; calculate by multiplying pounds by reps by sets (i.e. if you do 5 sets of 15 reps of 40 lbs on an exercise your volume is 40*15*5 = 3000 lbs, you want that to be greater or equal in your future lifts over time)

Alcohol:

  • Don't drink the same day or the day after training (exercising breaks down muscles and alc will be processed first delaying MPS)
  • Alcohol increases cortisol and estrogen levels (and decreases T, maybe a positive for women idk lol)
  • Still have a good time tho and be moderate

Physiology:

  • Know the function of each muscle you wanna train
  • Know how to activate each muscle (practice flexing no joke)
  • Follow the fibers when training (use basic physics)

Fat Loss:

  • Lose subcutaneous fat by caloric deficit
  • Only do light cardio after working out if any (15 min) cuz cardio can be catabolic
  • Do HIIT for visceral fat loss, you can lose visceral fat even in a cal surplus
  • Fats don't necessarily make you fat
  • Can't spot reduce fat
  • To show abs lower bf%
  • Abs #-pack is genetic
  • Do stomach vacuums to temporarily control waist size
  • [Need more research on this one]Bloating/bloated abs is caused by gut microbiome (SCFAs) & diet (foods broken down release gas) and eat lots of fibers, Don't eat super large meals in any one sitting, Don't use a low-FODMAP diet long-term, Eat prebiotic foods

Supplements/Diet:

  • Macros are important but so are micros so eat ur fruits & veggies lil bro
  • Eat 0.8g protein per lb at least
  • Creatine Monohydrate (can't get naturally ez and has been thoroughly studied)
  • Pre-Workout (avoid DMAA/DMHA), Try to cycle on and off so your body doesn't change its equilibrium
  • Ashwaganda (I don't use this but has been proven to boost T and strength and *brown flexing emoji*)
  • Turmeric (*brown flexing emoji*)

Misc Tips:

  • Anabolic window doesn't exist
  • Learn benefits of cold/hot showers
  • Learn terms & phrases - DOMS, RPE, Anabolic, Androgenic, etc.
  • Listen to science and studies, not anecdotes from juicers
  • Stay natural unless you're competing (hormones, organ health, and it's basically cheating)

2 Compound Sets: 5x2 Reps Warm Up - Bar and 50% 1RM, 4x8 Sets x Reps Working - 75% 1RM RPE 7/8, Control Eccentric
